Editing a contact

  1. Navigate to the Contact screen from the left navigation or from any lookup/search and search for a specific Contact which he/she wants to edit/update.

  2. Click the Edit icon from the list screen to be redirected to the Edit screen for Contacts

  3. Click the Edit button to enable the edit mode within the form.

  4. Make the desired changes in the fields and click the Submit button.

  5. Once submitted, a summary page is displayed with a view of the changes.

  6. Click on the Confirm button, to update the contact data.

  7. [optional] Download as PDF by clicking the icon on the Edit and Create Contact summary screen to download it. customer, contact entities.
