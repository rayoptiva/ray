Editing a contact

  1. Navigate to the Contact screen by clicking the icon on the left navigation or from the search box, search for a specific contact which you want to edit or update.

  2. Click the Edit icon on the Contact list screen to be redirected to the Edit Contact screen for Contacts.

  3. Click the Edit button or select a contact to enable the edit mode within the form.

  4. Make the desired changes in the fields and click the Submit button.

  5. Once submitted, a summary page is displayed with a view of the changes.

  6. Click on the Confirm button, to update the contact data.

  7. [Optional] Download as PDF by clicking the icon on the Edit and Create Contact summary screen to download it.
