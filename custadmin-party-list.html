Party

A Party is an individual that is used to represent a customer

The party is used to create a customer in the application.

  • The Party is created to record an individual before the assignment of any role.
    • The individual can be a customer, an employee or any other person that the organization needs to store information about.

Search for an existing Party or Create a new Party

Go to Creating a party for instructions.

You can also click > New Party on the dashboard.
