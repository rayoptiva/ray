Creating a party

Create a Party to use as basis for creating a customer

Create the Party (Individual) to record an individual before the assignment of any role.

Organization Party is available in the menu under but is not available for use at this time.

  • From the Dashboard click > New Party
  • From the left Navigation click Party > > Individual Party

Click on each tab to populate and save the relevant details:

  • Individual
  • Contacts
  • Individual Identification
  • Credit Profile
  • Tax Exemption Certificate
  • Additional Information
Note: At any time during the data entry process, you can click Cancel to keep as draft or delete the record.

Individual

Note: Given Name and Family Name are required fields.
  1. Complete the Basic Information and Personal Information sections.
  2. [Optional] Add another name if needed.
  3. Click Save.

Contacts

  1. Click the Contacts tab.
  2. Click + Add Contact to add a new contact.
  3. Add Contacts and select Preferred to make this option the preferred contact option.
  4. Click Save.

Indivdual Identification

  1. Click the Individual Identification tab.
  2. Click + Add Identification.
  3. Add identification information.
  4. Upload any supporting ID
  5. Select Validated to validate this party.
    • To mark as Validated at a later stage, select Mark Validated on the Actions menu for this entry.
  6. Click Save.

Credit Profile

  1. Click the Credit Profile tab.
  2. Click + Add Credit Rating.
  3. Input credit rating information.
  4. Click Save.

Tax Exemption Certificate

  1. Click the Tax Exemption Certificate tab.
  2. Click + Add Tax Certificate.
  3. Input relevant information.
  4. Upload the tax certificate.
  5. Click Save.

Additional Information

  1. Click the Additional Information tab.
  2. Click Add for the following sections to add specific information.
    • Language Ability
    • Skill
    • Disability
    • External Reference
    • Additiona Information
  3. Click Save when you add information.

Submit and Confirm

When you've completed adding information for all the sections:

  1. Click Submit to submit the data.
  2. [Optional] Click the Edit button for any section to make any changes before you confirm the data.
  3. Review the submitted content and then click Confirm.
