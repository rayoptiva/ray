Creating a party - Organization

Create a Party to use as basis for creating a customer that represents an organizaiton

Create the Party to record an organization before the assignment of any role.

  1. Click the party icon on the left navigation bar to open the Party page.

  2. Click the + Create button to create a party.

  3. Select Organization and click Confirm.

  4. Complete the following forms by selecting the relevant tabs on the right and save.

    Organization
    Contact
    1. Click the Contacts tab.
    2. Click Add to add a new contact.
    3. Add Contacts and select Preferred to make this option the preferred contact option.
    4. Click Save.
    Individual Identification
    1. Click the Individual Identification tab.
    2. Click Add.
    3. Add identification information,
    4. Upload any supporting ID
    5. Select Validated to validate this party.
    Credit Profile
    1. Click the Credit Profile tab.
    2. Click + Add Credit Rating.
    3. Input credit rating information and click Save.
    Tax Exemption Certificate
    1. Click the Tax Exemption Certificate tab.
    2. Click + Add Tax Certificate.
    3. Input relevant information and upload the tax certificate.
    4. Click Save.
    Additional Information
    1. Click the Additional Information tab.
    2. Click Add for the following sections to add specific information.
      • Language Ability
      • Skill
      • Disability
      • External Reference
      • Additiona Information
    3. Click Save when you add information.
    • Language Ability
    • Skill
    • Disability
    • External Reference
    • Additiona Information
  5. Click Submit to submit the data.
  6. Review the submitted content and then click Confirm.
