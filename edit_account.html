Edit Account

Edit an account.

  1. Access the Accounts page by clicking the Account icon on the left navigation bar.
  2. On the Accounts page, search for the account that you want to edit.
    Note: Click the eye icon to view account dashboard for a selected account.
  3. Select the target account and click the edit icon to open this account for editing.
    1. Make your required changes.
    2. Click Save > Update > Confirm.
