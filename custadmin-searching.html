Dashboard

Manage your customer administration from this dashboard.

Do the following tasks from the dashboard.

Search

Type a search term such as a customer name or order number in the search box and click the search icon. Select any of the following categories to filter your search.

  • All
  • Accounts
  • Subscriptions
  • Transactions
  • Customer
  • Party
  • Contact
  • Orders
  • Cases

Top Navigation Bar

Click the icons or menu options on the top bar to do the following:

  • Select the Telecom provider
  • Select a language
  • Check Notifications
  • Check Messages

Creating new entities

Click the + Create button in the upper right to create any of the following entities:

  • New Order
  • New Case
  • New Party
  • New Customer
  • New Account
  • New Contact

Configuring settings

Click the icon to configure settings on your dashboard.
