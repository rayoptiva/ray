Editing Customers

Edit a customer record on the Customer page.

Each customer is associated with a particular party which you can add or select through search when you create the customer.

Edit a Customer Record

  1. Click the Customer icon on the left navigation panel.

  2. Search for an existing customer by using the search box or click the advanced search icon to open an advanced search dialog.

  3. Filter your customer search using the following list filters.

    1. List all customers

    2. List drafts for customers

    3. List approved customers
  4. [Optional] Customize the fields and order of the displayed results by clicking the icons.
  5. Click the icons under Actions to make changes to a selected customer.
    • Click the eye icon () to view the customer dashboard.
    • Click the pencil icon to edit a specific customer. You can't edit the party details.
      • Edit a section and click Update and Confirm to confirm changes.
    • Click the elipisis (…) to get options to
      • Approve a customer.
      • Delete the customer. (Only available for drafts)
