Creating a party

Create a Party to use as basis for customer creation

Create the Party to record an individual or an organization before the assignment of any role.

  1. Click the create party icon on the left navigation bar,

  2. Select Individual or Organization and click Confirm.

  3. Depending on your selection, complete and Save the following forms.

    Individual Organization
    Individual Organization
    Contact Contact
    Individual Identification ID
    Credit Rating ?
    Tax Exemption Certificate ?
    Language Ability ?
    Skill ?
    Disability ?
    Additional Information ?
    External Reference ?
  4. Click Submit to submit the data.
  5. Review the submitted content and then click Confirm.
