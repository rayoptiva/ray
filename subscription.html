Subscriptions

A subscription entitles a customer to an automatic and prescribed public mobile service from the provider.

To open the subscriptions page, click Subscriptions on the left navigation.

Search subscriptions

Use the following options to search and modify subscriptions.
  • Select Prepaid or Postpaid.
  • Search by Account ID or BAN.
    • Filter your search by clicking the following filter icons:

      1. Mobile
      2. Landline
      3. Ethernet
      4. IPTV
    • Click the advanced filter to search by specific options.
  • Click Recharge on the subscription card to recharge this subscription.
  • Click the ellipis (…) on the top right of the subscription card to open menu for Recharge history, Invoice history, Change plan, Pin management, and several more depending on the subscription.
  • Click My Plan on the subscription card to view subscription details.
    • View Data details
    • View SMS details
    • View Call details
    • View Flex bundles details
    • Click Usage Details to view the usage.

      View usage details for calls, SMS, and data.

    • Click Change Plan to change the plan.
    • Click Recharge to recharge.

      The Recharge option is also available from the Usage Details section.

  • Click the ellipisis (…) on a subscription card to open a menu:
    • Recharge History
    • Subscription History
    • Add Ons
    • Deactivate
    • Change Plan
    • Closed User Groups
