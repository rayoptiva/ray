Editing Closed User Groups

Go to the Closed User Groups (CUG) screen. Use search to find Private and Public GUGs.

Use the advanced search to search by CUG Type, CUG Name, or Short Code.

  1. Click the eye icon under Actions to view a CUG.

  2. On the Public or Private CUG dialog screen, click the eye icon next to the CUG member to view member details.
  3. Click the Edit icon that corresponds with a member under CUG members.
    • Search, edit, view details, or delete GUG members.
  4. Click Update when you finish making changes.
  5. To delete a member, click the delete icon next to the member and then click Yes to confirm.
