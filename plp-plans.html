Personal List Plans

[Draft - Placeholder]

The Personal List Plan (PLP) enables the provider to give prepaid and postpaid subscriptions with preferential rates to the subscriber when they place mobile-originated (MO) to or receive mobile-terminated (MT) calls from numbers that are provisioned as “friends and family” numbers or members of a special group.

For a personal list plan, the discount applies to the plan owner when the plan owner makes the call or receives the calls from the numbers in the PLP list.

Viewing Personal List Plans

  1. Click Subscriptions on the left navigation panel.
  2. Select a subscription or search for a subscription.
  3. Click the ellipis (…) to open the menu and select Personal List Plans.
